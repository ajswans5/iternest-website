# IterNest Website Brief

## Primary job of the website

Help a visitor quickly understand:

1. What IterNest is.
2. What products are being built.
3. Why the company exists.
4. How to follow the build journey or get in touch.

## Core message

IterNest builds thoughtful software that reduces cognitive load. The products should help people see the situation clearly before making decisions.

## Experience principles

- Summary first; details second.
- Understanding before recommendations.
- Human language instead of technical language.
- Strong mobile experience.
- Generous whitespace and calm hierarchy.
- No unnecessary animation, clutter, or competing calls to action.

## Initial page map

- `/` — Home
- `/products` — Product overview
- `/products/homeschool` — IterNest for Homeschool
- `/products/entrepreneurs` — IterNest for Entrepreneurs
- `/products/moneymap` — MoneyMap
- `/journal` — Journal index / From the Nest
- `/journal/[slug]` — Individual journal post
- `/about` — Founder and company story
- `/contact` — Contact path
- `/privacy` — Privacy policy
- `/404` — Not found

## Homepage priority

The newest journal post should be easy to find near the top of the homepage. Older posts should remain available through the Journal archive rather than filling the homepage.
