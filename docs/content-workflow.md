# Journal Publishing Workflow

## Source of truth

Journal drafts live in `content/journal/` as Markdown files.

## Naming convention

Use:

```text
YYYY-MM-DD-post-slug.md
```

Example:

```text
2026-07-01-why-im-starting-with-homeschool-families.md
```

## Required metadata

Each post should include:

- title
- date
- slug
- excerpt
- status (`draft` or `published`)

## Publishing behavior

- Published posts appear on the Journal index.
- The newest published post appears on the homepage.
- Draft posts never appear publicly.
- Posts should preserve AJ's natural founder voice and avoid generic AI marketing language.
