About

IterNest builds calm tools for real life.

The company exists to reduce mental load by helping people understand the situation before choosing the next step.

IterNest began with the practical weight of planning, building, learning, and deciding while real life keeps moving.

The first products focus on homeschool families, entrepreneurs, and money decisions. Those areas look different on the surface, but they share the same need: clarity before action.

The work is being built in public through From the Nest, the IterNest founder journal.
